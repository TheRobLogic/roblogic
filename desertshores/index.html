The Short Version

Here's What We're Doing

Your videos and photos are already good. The problem is nobody sees them because you're only posting a couple times a month, and most of your best work ends up on the agent's feed instead of yours.

We're going to fix that by turning your Instagram into your portfolio. Every property you shoot goes up. Not just the ones you're proud of, all of them. Mixed in with some Lake Havasu lifestyle stuff, First Friday, lake sunsets, local events. That way your feed shows off both your work and the place you're selling.

The whole thing runs on about 3 hours a week. One batch session where you knock out all your posts, then 15 minutes a day replying to comments and liking other people's stuff. That's it.

The rest of this doc is the playbook. Hashtags, captions, templates, the whole thing. You don't need to memorize it, just use it as a reference when you're posting. We'll circle back and adjust as we go.

First Things First

Your Next 5 Steps

Do these in order. Each one takes 15-30 minutes tops.

Step 1 Update your bio and name field

Copy the bio from page 5. Change your name field to include "RE Photo & Video" so people searching for that find you.

Step 2 Set up a link-in-bio page

Go to beacons.ai, make a free page. Add your HD Photohub portfolio link, a pricing page if you have one, and a way to contact you. Put that link in your Instagram bio.

Step 3 Build your CapCut Reel template

Make one Reel with the formula on page 4: text hook at the start, property footage in the middle, your logo at the end. Save it as a template so you can just swap footage next time.

Step 4 Post your first week of content

Pick your best recent shoots. Make 3 Reels, 1 carousel, and 1 hero shot. Use the hashtags from page 5. Post them or schedule them with Later (free).

Step 5 Start the daily 15 minutes

Reply to any comments, like some local real estate posts, and see what's getting views. That's all.

That's the whole system

Once you've done those 5 things, you're in the rhythm. The rest of this doc is reference material for when you need to look up hashtags, caption ideas, or what to post next. We'll check in and see how it's going.

02 — The Strategy

What We're Building

A self-powered Instagram engine. No dependency on agents, partnerships, or paid ads. Travis posts, Travis grows. If agents engage — bonus, not requirement.

Key Insight

Buyers aren't locals moving around Lake Havasu — they're relocating from all over the country. Your content needs to travel beyond LHC. The Reels algorithm doesn't care about follower count; it cares about watch time and engagement. One well-hooked drone reveal can reach 50K+ people from a 42-follower account.

Three Pillars

  1. Post everything. If you shoot it, it goes on Instagram. The grid is the portfolio. Volume + consistency beats perfection.
  2. Mix in lifestyle. First Friday, lake life, local events — this is what out-of-state buyers are actually shopping for. It also reaches people outside real estate hashtags.
  3. Go broad. National real estate hashtags + Arizona lifestyle tags + local LHC tags. Cast a wide net, let the algorithm find your audience.

Reel Formula (CapCut Template)

1. Hook text overlay (first 1 sec) — "This lake house was listed with iPhone photos..."
2. Property footage (15-25 sec) — walkthrough, drone, or reveal
3. End card — logo + "Desert Shores Media" + location
+ Text overlays throughout (85% of video is watched on mute)
+ Trending audio from Instagram's Creative Audio library

Caption Formula

  1. Hook (line 1): Earn the "more" tap — "This listing sat on Zillow with 4 iPhone photos. Here's what happened after."
  2. Story: What the shoot delivered — "Delivered full gallery + drone by 5pm same day."
  3. Soft CTA: "DM me for pricing" or "Link in bio for packages"

Lifestyle Content Ideas

  • First Friday — short Reel walking the event, vendors, vibe
  • Local events — boat shows, lake sunsets, downtown, seasonal happenings
  • "Why Havasu" moments — 15-second clips that sell the town, not a property
  • Can be shot on phone anytime — zero dependency on having a booking that week

06 — Weekly Workflow

The 3-Hour Week

Everything fits in a few hours. One batch session plus short daily check-ins.

Batch Day — 1.5 hours

Pick one day per week

  1. Pull footage/photos from that week's shoots
  2. Cut 3 Reels in CapCut using your template (~15 min each)
  3. Pick 1 carousel set + 1 hero image
  4. Write captions (hook + property + location + CTA)
  5. Schedule all 5 posts for the week

Daily Micro-Sessions — 15 min/day

5 days = 1.25 hours total

  1. Reply to comments and DMs
  2. Like/comment on 5-10 posts in LHC real estate hashtags
  3. Note what's performing — what gets views, saves, shares
Mindset Shift

This isn't a creative project where only the best work goes up. It's a marketing machine. The algorithm rewards accounts that post regularly — 5x/week tells Instagram "this account is active, show it to people." Volume + consistency beats perfection every time.

About Paid Ads

Boosting from the Instagram app is Meta's worst ad product — it burns money with minimal targeting. When you're ready for paid, we'll use Meta Ads Manager with proper targeting: Lake Havasu + 15-mile radius, real estate occupation, Zillow/Realtor Magazine interests, and custom audiences from your client list. At $150-300/month in a 60K-population city, you'd get near-total name recognition among active agents in 60-90 days.
