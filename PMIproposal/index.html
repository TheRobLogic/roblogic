01 — The Opportunity

Cross-Border Pet Care Is Exploding — and PetMex Is Perfectly Positioned

Pet medical tourism from San Diego to Tijuana isn’t a fringe idea anymore. It’s a fast-growing market covered by ABC News and Good Morning America, with costs 50–90% lower than US equivalents.

Key Market Data

33,500+ pets crossed the US-Mexico border for vet care in 2022 — a 68% increase from 2019. An estimated 80% of clients at top Tijuana clinics are Americans.

Why This Matters for PetMex

Demand is surging, but most pet owners searching online for this service will find MexiVet Express before they find PetMex. Not because MexiVet is better — they’re not — but because they have better SEO, more reviews, and a clearer website.

PetMex has the better product. It just needs the digital presence to match.

The Core Problem

Right now, when a San Diego pet owner Googles “pet transport Tijuana vet” at 11pm, they find MexiVet. They never know PetMex exists. Our job is to change that — without changing what makes PetMex special.

05 — The AI + Human Touch Promise

AI Handles the Waiting. Humans Handle the Caring.

We know your clients love PetMex because of you — Lizette and TJ, by name. We would never replace that. Here’s our framework for what gets automated and what stays human:

Automate (AI Territory)
  • ⚡ After-hours inquiry capture — bot collects info, Lizette calls back next morning
  • ⚡ Review request texts (24hrs after service)
  • ⚡ Appointment reminder texts
  • ⚡ Social media post scheduling
  • ⚡ FAQ chatbot (top 10 questions)
  • ⚡ Lead intake form organization
  • ⚡ Competitor activity monitoring
Always Human (Non-Negotiable)
  • ❤️ The first phone call — always Lizette
  • ❤️ Pickup and delivery — always TJ
  • ❤️ Pricing discussions and quotes
  • ❤️ Responding to concerns or negative feedback
  • ❤️ Instagram DMs that need nuance
  • ❤️ Vet coordination and medical decisions
  • ❤️ Building client relationships

AI-Assisted (Human in the Loop)

These tasks use AI to draft, but Lizette reviews and sends:

  • Social media reply drafts — AI writes a response, Lizette taps “send” or edits. Her voice, her choice.
  • Blog/content drafts — AI writes “5 Things to Know Before Your Pet’s First Trip to TJ,” Lizette adds her real stories.
  • Email follow-ups — Personalized check-ins drafted by AI, approved by Lizette before sending.
The Promise

When someone finds PetMex at 11pm, the AI captures their info and answers their questions. When they wake up, Lizette calls them. They never talk to a robot when they need a person. They never wait in silence when a robot could help.

07 — Review Growth Engine

From 59 to 200+ Reviews in 6 Months

PetMex’s 5.0 rating is its crown jewel. But 59 reviews vs. MexiVet’s 369 means MexiVet looks more established at first glance. Here’s how we close that gap fast.

Automated Review Request System

24 hours after every service, an automated text goes out:

“Hi [Name]! This is PetMex — how’s [Pet] doing after yesterday? We loved taking care of them. If you have a moment, a quick Google review helps other pet families find us: [link]. Thank you! — Lizette & TJ”

Why This Works

  • Timing: 24 hours post-service = peak gratitude and relief
  • Personal: Uses the pet’s name, signed by Lizette & TJ
  • Frictionless: One tap to the review page
  • Consistent: Every client, every time — no manual remembering

At PetMex’s current volume, this system alone could generate 15–25 new reviews per month, reaching 200+ within six months while maintaining the perfect 5.0 rating.

Additional Trust Building

  • Apply for BBB accreditation (MexiVet has A+ — PetMex should too)
  • Create a Yelp business page and claim it
  • If applicable, highlight any federal licensing or insurance on the website
