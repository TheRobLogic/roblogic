We’ve Heard Them All

Yes, absolutely. Thousands of San Diego pet owners take their pets to Tijuana for veterinary care every year. In 2022 alone, over 33,500 pets crossed the border from the US to Mexico. PetMex handles all documentation and border-crossing logistics for you.
Our partner clinics employ board-certified veterinarians trained to the same standards as US vets. Many have trained in the US or Europe. We personally vet every clinic we work with — your pet only goes to clinics we trust with our own animals.
Our team stays with your pet the entire time. We provide real-time updates and translate every finding from the vet directly to you. If there are any concerns, we call you immediately so you can make decisions together with the doctor.
It depends on the procedure. A simple consultation or vaccine visit can be done in a few hours. Dental cleanings or minor surgeries are typically same-day. Major surgeries may require an overnight stay. We give you a time estimate before every visit.
We primarily transport dogs and cats, but we’ve handled other companion animals too. Each pet rides individually in our clean, climate-controlled vehicle — never kenneled, never crowded. Call us to discuss your pet’s needs.
Our service is designed so you don’t have to miss work or rearrange your day. However, if you’d like to accompany your pet, talk to us — we can work something out depending on the appointment.
You don’t need anything — we handle it all. PetMex takes care of all border-crossing documentation and veterinary paperwork. We organize everything so your pet’s trip is smooth and legal.
