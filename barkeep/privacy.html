BarKeep App — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

BarKeep ("the App") is a private, internal-use application operated by Bancroft Bar LLC. This policy describes how the App handles data accessed through QuickBooks Online.

2. Data Collected

The App reads financial data from QuickBooks Online, including but not limited to: chart of accounts, profit and loss reports, balance sheets, invoices, bills, and journal entries. The App does not collect personal information from end users or customers.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used solely for internal business analysis by authorized personnel of Bancroft Bar LLC. Data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Data accessed through QuickBooks Online is processed locally on secure devices controlled by Bancroft Bar LLC. OAuth tokens are stored locally with restricted file permissions. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond the QuickBooks Online API.

5. Data Retention

The App does not maintain a persistent copy of QuickBooks data. Data is queried on-demand and not cached beyond the current session.

6. Security

OAuth credentials are stored with restricted file permissions (chmod 600). Access to the App is limited to authorized devices.

7. Third-Party Services

The App connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's official OAuth 2.0 API. No other third-party services are used.
