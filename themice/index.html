[ BIO ]

Rob Logic // vocals, guitar
Travis Poe // vocals, bass
Raul Campuzano // drums

California trio contributes standard guitar/bass/drums Punk-Pop, stretching from quieter, decidedly Pop tunes to upbeat Punk, periodically throwing a little Funk around.

Originally Billy Club (~1996). Matt went to rehab and never came back. When Matt's away the Mice will play.

[ PRESS ]

"The Mice manage to combine the drunken, youthful, vaguely poppy exuberance of early Replacements records with the obnoxious sing-along poppy punk of bands like the Offspring."

-- Listen.com

"These spiders, sorry, Mice have a nasty, frenzied, and often melodic sound... These creatures have heart, and that's what ultimately makes Volume 1 worth checking out."

-- Ben Hunter, Askew Reviews

"This 'punk' band from SD plays dirty sounding pop songs, that might sound like Ramones if on a 33 speed record played at 45 rpm. I hear a bit of Dinosaur Jr. in there someplace."

-- Wiretap

"The mice rock! From their great indie hooks and pop melodies to their more traditional alt-rock and punk arrangements, there is something here for everybody. This is one of those bands that truly does get better with age."

-- Juicy Jen

"The vocal range of frontman Rob Logic wavers between flat and monotone."

-- San Diego Reader, Sept 8, 2005
