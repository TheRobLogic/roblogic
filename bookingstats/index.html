Quick Wins

  1. Fill every open Friday and Saturday — each empty weekend night = ~$660 in lost revenue
  2. Book more Thursdays — only 44% filled, $396 lift per show
  3. Push 4-band bills — 35% more revenue than solo acts
  4. Respond to real inquiries faster — 26 bands played despite being ghosted, many more gave up
  5. Auto-filter agency spam — 40% of inbound, 0% converts, wastes time
