What is Plex?

Think of it as Netflix, but it's Rob's personal movie and TV collection. You get a free account, I share my libraries with you, and you stream whatever you want.

🖥

Pin Your Libraries

Pinning a library puts it right on your home screen so you don't have to dig for it every time.

  1. Click More at the bottom of the left sidebar
  2. Find the server's libraries in the list
  3. Click the ... menu next to a library and hit Pin
  4. Drag pinned libraries to reorder them however you like
🔄

Switching Between Servers

Both servers show up in the left sidebar. Just click a different server's library to switch. No logout or settings change needed.

📶

Remote Streaming

Plex changed their policy in 2025: streaming from outside your home network now requires a Remote Watch Pass ($2/mo or $20/yr). This is a Plex thing, not something I can control or get around. If you're on the same Wi-Fi as the server, it's still free.

💡

Tips

  • ✨ Set playback quality to Original or Maximum in settings for the best picture and sound.
  • 📱 Use the Plex app on your phone, tablet, or smart TV. It works way better than the browser.
  • ▶️ Continue Watching shows up on your home screen and works across both servers, so you won't lose your place.
