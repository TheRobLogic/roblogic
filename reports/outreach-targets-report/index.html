Methodology Notes

  • Booker detection: First band listed in event title (before first /) assumed to be the
    show booker. Excluded from outreach since they likely already have a bar relationship.
  • Email matching: thefuzz library with ratio + token_set_ratio scoring. Threshold: 80
    for names > 8 chars, 90 for shorter names (to reduce false positives).
  • Band dedup: Near-identical names merged at 88% fuzzy similarity.
  • Excluded events: Dart tournaments, HOLDs, Arrt Nite, private events, sports, karaoke.
  • Excluded band names: TBA, TBD, DPI, DAB, DJ, and other placeholders/abbreviations.
  • Revenue data: Toast POS daily sales 2022-2025. Shows before 2022 have no sales match.
