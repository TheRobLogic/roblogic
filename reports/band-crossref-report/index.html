Methodology

Band Name Extraction

  • Wix Forms: Parsed Band Name: field from @wixforms.com form submissions
  • Indie on the Move: Extracted from subject/body patterns (booking requests, hold requests, tour routing, artist pitches)
  • Agency emails: Parsed artist names from White Mountain Agency and Universal Attractions subject lines
  • Booking agents: Matched emails from known indie booking agents (Happy Nomad, Road Dog, SuperKool, Dream Desire, FractoFlow)
  • Promoter direct: Woke Up On a Throne -- recurring promoter/band with own domain
  • Cold/direct email: Identified booking-related subjects/body from all other senders; extracted names from subject patterns and sender display names

Fuzzy Matching

  • thefuzz library, token_sort_ratio scorer, threshold 82
  • Matched against 2025 show titles (split on / delimiter) and full band directory
  • Leading articles (the/a/an) tested both with and without

Noise Filtering

  • Extracted names filtered against patterns for subject-line fragments (e.g., "Playing a", "Booking a", "Availability for", date strings)
  • Spam/commercial domains excluded (Amazon, TickPick, LinkedIn, Uber, BMI, etc.)
  • Panda Artist Management excluded (Spanish-language PR newsletter)
  • Giggs.live excluded (platform marketing)
  • Wix marketing emails (pb01.wixemails.com) excluded; only form submissions included
  • Bancroft's own outgoing emails excluded

Limitations

  • Heuristic extraction: some false positives and false negatives remain
  • Multi-band lineups may only match one act from an email thread
  • Phone, text, Instagram DM, and Facebook bookings are invisible to this analysis
  • Agency blast emails are mass-sent to hundreds of venues and do not represent genuine booking interest
  • Some Wix submissions use event names rather than band names
  • Fuzzy matching may produce incorrect matches at boundary scores (82-85)
