Bancroft Bar — Venue Context for Booking Agent

Revenue Model

  • All door/ticket money goes to the bands — the bar takes zero from ticket sales
  • The bar makes money on DRINKS — more bodies in the room = more bar revenue
  • Therefore: the #1 metric for a "good booking" is DRAW (how many people show up)
  • Average spend per guest: ~$16 (stable across years)
  • Free shows can still be great if the band brings a crowd

Venue Details

  • 130 person capacity (can push to 150), dive bar in Spring Valley, San Diego
  • Live music venue — punk, metal, stoner/doom, garage/psych, goth/darkwave, indie rock, hip-hop
  • Ticketing through TickPick (online advance sales)
  • Also has door sales (cash/card at the door) — NOT captured in TickPick data
  • POS: Toast (Standard API Access, read-only, credentials in bancroft-bar.md)

Booking Process (current)

  • Email is the primary channel — ~85-91% of booked shows have email trail
  • One band/promoter books the night via email, then brings the rest of the bill
  • Wix form on website ("Band Booking Form") — highest converting channel (23%)
  • Cold emails from bands/promoters — ~16% conversion
  • Indie on the Move platform — 2% conversion
  • Agency pitches — 0% conversion (auto-reject)
  • Local promoters/curators who build multi-band bills
  • Main booking email: bancroftbar@gmail.com

Business Goal

  • Increase bar revenue by 30% — from ~$227K net (2024) to ~$295K
  • Automate booking — take most of the work off Kayla's plate, Rob + AI booker handle it
  • Revenue declined ~9% from 2023 ($250K net) to 2024 ($227K net)
  • Guest counts dropped 15% (16,654 → 14,112) — this is a foot traffic problem
  • Show-night revenue holds steady at ~$948/night. Non-show nights: ~$443
  • Shows add $505 incremental revenue per night — the lifeline

Revenue Levers (from data, QA-verified)

  1. Fill empty Fri/Sat nights — 15 unfilled in 2025 × $505 incremental = ~$5K
  2. Expand Thu bookings — only 36% fill rate, $355/show lift = ~$8.5K/year at 77%
  3. Kill the blues jam — losing ~$200/week vs a dark Wednesday = +$9.4K
  4. 4-band bills standard — $1,106 avg vs $819 solo (+35%)
  5. Auto-reject agency spam — 40% of inbound, 0% converts, wastes time
  6. Faster replies on real inquiries — 41% of Wix form leads get ghosted
  7. Proactive outreach — 39 bands identified that played on good nights but never contacted us
