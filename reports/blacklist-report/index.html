Patterns

The Bass Player Curse: At least 4 separate cancellations caused by bass players (surgery, sick, pneumonia, early shift). Not actionable but notable.

Summer is peak flake season: July-August has the highest cancellation cluster. Bands overcommit in summer.

Touring bands cancel for logistics, locals cancel for drama: Touring flakes = tour fell through, vehicle problems, routing changes. Local flakes = band member issues, competing gigs, internal drama.

Most cancellations are one-offs with legitimate reasons. No repeat serial flakers found — the blacklist is short because most bands handle cancellations professionally.
