Patterns in Who Gets Ghosted

  1. Wix forms are a black hole. 165 ghosted. Links get mangled through Wix/SendGrid — music links are often unclickable. The form itself may be hurting conversion.

  2. "We played here before" still gets ghosted. The highest-value leads (returning bands) get the same non-response as cold pitches.

  3. IOTM has bad signal-to-noise but some gems. 130 IOTM emails ghosted. Most are untargeted (country, jazz, singer-songwriter), which probably trained the inbox to ignore IOTM. But some were well-targeted (Abolishment of Flesh, Stay Out, Ghost Heart).

  4. Persistent bookers get zero acknowledgment. Happy Nomad sent 35 emails. Not even a "no thanks." This burns bridges with professional booking agents.

What the AI Booker Fixes

  1. Same-day triage. Every inquiry gets classified within hours, not weeks.
  2. Auto-reply for real leads. "Thanks for reaching out. We're reviewing your submission and will get back to you within 48 hours."
  3. Auto-reject for spam/mismatches. Template polite decline for country/jazz/agency spam.
  4. Flag returning bands. Any band that's played before gets priority.
  5. Fix the Wix form. Either fix the link mangling or replace with a direct email prompt.
  6. Track every lead. No more bands submitting twice with no response.
