Hip-hop/rap is the emerging opportunity. MC Chris ($2,107), Thee Rapscallions ($1,943) prove the genre works at Bancroft.

Goth/darkwave has a volume-vs-quality problem. DarkWave Garden went from 2 shows to 22, but per-show revenue dropped from $3,003 to $734. The genre isn't weak — it's being diluted by a low-revenue weekly format.
