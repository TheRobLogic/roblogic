What it does

New licenses, on your radar

Every morning the pipeline pulls fresh data straight from the California ABC, geocodes each business, and publishes an interactive map and searchable table.

Interactive Map

Every license plotted on an OpenStreetMap. Color-coded dots. Tap a pin to see the full details and jump to Google Maps.

Filter & Search

Toggle by category — on-premise, off-premise, wholesale, other. Search by name, address, or city. Drill into specific license types.

Daily Refresh

Automated pipeline runs every morning. New licenses appear automatically — no manual updates, no stale data.

How to use it

Five taps to your next lead

Pick a category

The four toggle buttons at the top filter the entire view — map and table. Tap one to focus on that license type. Tap it again to show all.

On-Premise Off-Premise Wholesale Other

Search or narrow down

Type a business name, street, or city into the search bar. Use the dropdown to filter by a specific license type (e.g., Type 47 — On-Sale General).

Tap a card or a pin

Each business shows up as a card below the map and a dot on the map. Tap a card and the map scrolls into view and highlights that location. Tap a map pin to see a popup with full details.

Get directions

Every card and popup has a Google Maps link. Tap it to open directions straight from your phone — or look up the business right there.

Make contact

When we have a phone number or website on file, it's right on the card. Tap the number to call, or the link to visit their site. More contact data enriches automatically over time.
