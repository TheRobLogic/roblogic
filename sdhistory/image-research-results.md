### Key Historical Facts (from UT article):
- Built in 1932
- Co-owned/operated by Andrew "Cactus" Soldi and Eugene "Smokey" Rogers, 1952-1963
- Smokey Rogers hosted KFMB TV show "Smokey Rogers' General Store"
- Performers: Hank Williams, Patsy Cline, Johnny Cash, Jerry Lee Lewis, Roy Orbison, Carl Perkins, Charley Pride, Merle Haggard, Willie Nelson, Bill Monroe, Rose Maddox, Bob Wills & His Texas Playboys, Roy Acuff, The Louvin Brothers, Marty Robbins, Flatt & Scruggs, Brenda Lee, Lefty Frizzell
- Chris Hillman (Rock & Roll Hall of Fame): "The Bostonia Ballroom was the big game in town for country and bluegrass music"
- Later became Royal Palace Banquet Hall

### Hank Williams Promotional Photo (for context)

- Shows: Hank Williams MGM Records promotional photo, 1948
- License: Public domain
